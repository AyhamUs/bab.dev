Privacy Policy

At Bab Games, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage your privacy preferences.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or play our games, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: If you contact us, sign up for newsletters, or interact with us, we may collect your name, email address, and any other details you provide.
  • Non-Personal Information: This includes anonymous data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and usage statistics of our website.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These technologies help us analyze how users interact with our site, and allow us to customize content and ads to better suit your preferences.

You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you prefer, you can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services such as Google AdSense and other analytics tools to serve ads and collect data. These third parties may collect data about your activities on our site and across the web in order to provide you with targeted ads.

For more information, please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party services:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense Privacy Policy

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve and personalize your gaming experience.
  • Show relevant ads based on your interests.
  • Monitor and analyze trends and performance of our website and games.
  • Respond to your inquiries or requests for support.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no data transmission over the internet or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure. We are not responsible for the security of information you transmit to us over the internet.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or updates to your personal data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data (subject to applicable legal obligations).
  • Opt-out of targeted advertising or the use of cookies, as described above.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Please review this page periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please feel free to contact us.
